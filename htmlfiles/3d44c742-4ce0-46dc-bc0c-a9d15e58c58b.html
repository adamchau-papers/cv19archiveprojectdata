Note: This episode contains strong language.

As the death toll from the coronavirus rises in the U.S., so do reports of verbal and physical attacks against Asian-Americans, who say hostile strangers are blaming them for the pandemic. Today, one writer shares her story.

On today’s episode:

  • Jiayang Fan, a staff writer at The New Yorker magazine.

Background reading:

  • Ms. Fan’s story is echoed across the country by others who say they have been spit on, yelled at and attacked. Asian-American community and political leaders have tried to comfort their constituents. But they, too, admit to feeling unnerved.

  • Some have turned to social media to share their stories and procure medical supplies in an effort to aid the crisis response.
